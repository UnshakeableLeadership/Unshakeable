The Framework

Step 0 — the moment before action

Under urgency, the amygdala fires, cortisol spikes, and the prefrontal cortex goes offline. Options narrow. Smart, capable people optimise for relief instead of quality.

Step 0 is the deliberate pause between automatic reaction and intentional decision. It is not a mindfulness practice or a productivity system. It is a decision condition reset.

The people who struggle most with AI and accelerating environments aren't the least capable. They are operating under the most pressure. That is a decision condition problem — not a willpower problem.

"Better decisions require better decision conditions."

01

Pause

20–30 minutes. Walk. Breathe. Not thinking harder — stopping the loop.

02

Orient

What decision am I actually making? What pressure is rushing me?

03

Decide

Apply one value as a constraint. Let values do the filtering.

04

Engage

Now act. Deliberately, not reactively.

05

Refine

Learn. What did this reveal about how you decide under pressure?

About

Built from the ground up.

I'm Sean Attwood — Canadian-born, London-based. In 2004 I co-founded Yumchaa, a loose leaf tea chain that grew from £4 on the first day to seven shops, twelve market stalls, and £2m in annual revenue. We took the company to a public listing.

Then we lost it. Investor fraud. The company was listed on an illiquid Cyprus exchange and systematically drained. The SEC and FSA both investigated. We walked away with nothing.

That experience — of building something real, losing it to forces outside our control, and choosing deliberately how to move forward — is the origin of everything I now teach.

For the past decade I've been delivering coaching and facilitation through MindGym and independently, across corporate leadership programmes, high-stakes teams, and executive development. 2,500+ sessions. 40,000+ people trained. The work is grounded in behavioural science. The authority behind it is lived.

The Unshakeable framework is the distillation: decision-making under pressure, and the conditions that make it possible.
